FAQ

How does iMate remain undetectable during interviews?
iMate uses advanced audio processing and discrete interface technology that operates seamlessly in the background, ensuring complete invisibility to interviewers while providing you with real-time assistance.
What types of interviews does iMate support?
iMate supports all types of interviews including technical interviews, behavioral interviews, case studies, and industry-specific assessments across various fields like technology, finance, healthcare, and more.
Is my data secure and private?
Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade encryption and follow strict privacy protocols. Your interview data is never stored permanently and is completely deleted after each session to ensure maximum privacy and security.
